Jalen's Favorite Games

We will be ranking my favorite games. Growing up I used to be a big gamer
More recently I have been getting back into it. So let's go through some of my favorites
from a young age to now. We will also include various information on the games

  1. Mario Super Sluggers

    An amazing game that I recenetly revisted and had an amazing time with. Considering how old the game is the graphics still looked good. The gameplay is intuitive and all the characters feel like they play different, even the color copies. The special hits/pitches have been revisted from the previous game. Only wish would be a more robust story mode that serves more purpose than mainly a storymode

    Year Released Platforms IGN Score
    2008 Wii 7.4/10

  2. WWE '12

    Huge WWE fan and love playing their games and WWE '12 was the one that did something special. Plenty of game modes, an amazing story mode, robust mode for creating storylines as well. Second game after their overhaul of the way their gaming works and it was an insanely good time

    Year Released Platforms IGN Score
    2011 PS3, Wii, Xbox 360 9/10

  3. Pokemon White

    I am a huge pokemon fan now, but when this game came out I had new played a pokemon game before. This game was truly amazing It introduced new battle types, it made online battling/trading so much easier, introduced a new pokedex and forced everyone to choose pokemon exclusive to the region, and most importantly This game easily has the best story of any pokemon game! This region was also the first not based off a region Japan

    Year Released Platforms IGN Score
    2010 Nintendo DS 9/10

  4. Marvel Ultimate Alliance

    A game I have played, many many times. With cheat codes, without cheatcodes, A game that's just really fun to play. Finding multiple combinations of characters to play with never gets tiring. This game introduced me to so many different characters and teams in the marvel universe. It's a game I could play now and still get tons of enjoyment of out it

    Year Released Platforms IGN Score
    2006 Xbox, PC, PS2, Xbox 360, GBA, PSP
    Wii, PS3, PS4, Xbox One    		 8.1/10

  5. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

    This is easily my favorite game ever. The fact that every character that has been in an iteration of Super Smash Bros is in the game is amazing. The roster is absolutely huge and almost everyone is super fun to play. A Game I have spent literally 1000+ hours playing and that number shall only continue to grow.

    2018 Nintendo Switch 9.4/10
